🚀 Excited to share our progress on **CivikSutra (The Civic Formula)** for the Hack2Skill Promptwards hackathon!

💡 **The Initial Idea:**
We are building an AI-powered election process education assistant tailored for Indian citizens. Our goal is to meet voters where they are—both physically and informationally—by helping them find their polling booths, research candidates with AI, compare party manifestos, and get step-by-step voting guides.

🗺️ **Planning Our Approach:**
Taking an "Architect First, AI Executes" approach, we didn't just start coding. We planned a scalable, productizable architecture using a robust tech stack (React PWA, Python FastAPI/Firebase Cloud Functions). We designed a strategic delivery plan:
1. Foundation: GPS-based booth finder.
2. Intelligence: Gemini-powered candidate research grounded in Google Search.
3. Depth: Manifesto comparisons and an AI Chat Assistant.
4. Polish: Multilingual support (Hindi, English, regional languages).

🤖 **Building with AI Module by Module:**
Using AI tools wasn't just about code completion; it was about "vibe coding" with intent-driven prompting:
- **Project Scaffolding:** AI helped set up our deployment pipelines, Dockerfiles, and project structure instantly.
- **Booth Finder:** We prompted AI to seamlessly integrate Google Maps API for real-time traffic and location data.
- **Candidate Intelligence:** We utilized Gemini 2.0 Flash to synthesize complex criminal, asset, and educational data into readable, comparative insights.
- **Multilingual Chat:** AI guided our implementation of translation pipelines, ensuring the app speaks the voter's language.

By acting as the Architect and letting AI execute the syntax, we built a secure, scalable, and responsive application for civic empowerment.
